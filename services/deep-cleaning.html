Advanced Commercial Deep Cleaning

Precision, industrial-grade deep cleaning solutions designed for high-standard corporate offices, retail spaces, and sprawling facilities.

Heavy-duty floor crystallization, stripping, sealing, and mirror-finish polishing. Restores natural granite, marble, tiles, vinyl, and industrial epoxy surfaces.

Carpet & Partition Care

Industrial pile agitation, injection shampooing, hot water extraction, and steam sterilisation for wall-to-wall office carpets, board chairs, and fabric partitions.
