Specialised Treatments

Advanced Commercial Deep Cleaning

Precision, industrial-grade deep cleaning solutions designed for high-standard corporate offices, retail spaces, and sprawling facilities.

Floor Restoration & Polish

Heavy-duty floor crystallization, stripping, sealing, and mirror-finish polishing. Restores natural granite, marble, tiles, vinyl, and industrial epoxy surfaces.

Carpet & Partition Care

Industrial pile agitation, injection shampooing, hot water extraction, and steam sterilisation for wall-to-wall office carpets, board chairs, and fabric partitions.

Medical-Grade Sanitisation

Full electrostatic chemical spraying and thorough micro-wipe down of high-traffic zones: elevator control panels, reception desks, door handles, and cafeteria zones.

High-Level HEPA Dusting

Telescopic vacuuming and soot removal of high ceilings, exposed HVAC ductwork, cable trays, structural beams, air diffusers, and skylights with zero dust dispersion.

Facade Glass Detailing

Streak-free squeegee polishing of high facade glass panes, vertical mirror columns, tall atrium panels, and clear corporate lobby glass walls.
